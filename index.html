Homage to My London Bike Life

I became a cycing commuter in London when I moved to East London. The trains in East London where connected by overground trains in order to access the main underground rail system. To compensate for the lack of direct access to the underground, there are an abumdance of buses to transport one to the underground station or to thier final destination. It didn't take me long to figure out, if I just rode my bike I could make it almost anywhere in London faster than public transit, so I became a cycling commuter, loved the freedom and saved tones of £
